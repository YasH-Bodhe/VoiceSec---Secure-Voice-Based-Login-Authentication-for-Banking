How It Works

1

Register

Create an account with your email and password, then record your voice sample.

2

Extract Embedding

Your voice is processed using WavLM to create a unique speaker embedding.

3

Login

Enter your email and speak to verify your identity.

4

Verify

System compares your voice with stored embedding using cosine similarity.

5

Anti-Spoof Check

Advanced heuristics detect and block replay or synthetic attacks.

6

Authenticate

Successful authentication grants access. OTP backup available if needed.
